About Us

Modern Technology, a company firmly established and rooted in Iraq, specialising in the field of Information Technology Infrastructure and Engineering Services. Our operations are managed and meticulously overseen by a team of highly experienced IT, Electrical, and Instrumentation engineers and consultants. With a rich history spanning over 15 years, our team has gained invaluable experience working with local, multinational, and international companies, equipping us with a broad perspective and a deep understanding of diverse business environments. Our vision is to ascend as a leading, trusted, and distinctive professional Information Technology and Engineering firm. We are committed to delivering sustainable benefits to our clients and our country. Our focus is on providing high-quality services that not only meet but exceed our clients expectations. We aspire to be a beacon of excellence in our field, setting new standards and driving innovation.
